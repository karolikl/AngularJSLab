Before we begin:

  • Complete newbie?

    It might be a good idea for you to watch Dan Wahlin's "AngularJS Fundamentals In 60-ish Minutes" video if you have no prior experience with AngularJS before beginning this lab.

  • Resources

    In the main menu, you will find important resources to help you out with this lab. The meaning of this lab is not only to show you what is possible when using AngularJS, but it's also for you to do some reading and searching for solutions on your own. So take a quick look at the resources found in the main menu!

  • Exercises

    You will find several exercises in this lab. Some require coding whilst other require you to look at code and see if you can understand what is going on. In other words, you will be extending this website on your own. Fun, fun, fun!

    Feel free to contact me on Twitter.
